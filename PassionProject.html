  • I tackled this project in April/May 2020 and decided to specifically devote my time to learning new features and mechanics in the Unreal Engine 4 that I could include in this game. Some of these features are listed below:



  • Within this project, I studied user interfaces; specifically how they function and how the player can interact with them. This video shows my working food and drink system that requires the player to pay in-game money to buy specific food and drink.



  • Furthermore, I looked into NPCs and made a communication screen to allow the player to talk to John the NPC. Depending on the player's choices, the friendship meter could increase or decrease. Once friends, John appears on the player's 'friends list'.



  • Finally, I looked into the day/night cycle. This was an important feature that made the world seem dynamic. When the transition occurs, the player's view switches to look at the sky so they can physically see the sun setting and the stars appearing.
